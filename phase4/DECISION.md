# Phase 4 Decision

**IMPLEMENTED, MODEL AND CALENDAR ADAPTERS DISABLED.**

The repository contains a bounded intelligence engine and booking concurrency layer. Live language quality, provider availability, calendar permissions and real appointment operations remain unproven.

## Reversal conditions

Disable the playbook or booking resource after any unsafe promise, emergency misrouting, stale-version execution, wrong-tenant booking, duplicate provider booking, or capacity overrun.
