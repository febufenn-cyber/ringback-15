# Phase 5 Assumptions

- Financial evidence reaches the ledger only after provider signature and tenant authorization checks.
- Currency conversion is not inferred or silently performed.
- Partners have no access without an active customer-approved assignment.
- Commission payout and tax handling remain external controlled operations.
- Cohort reports require at least three distinct organizations by default.
- Historical Phase 0–4 records are backfilled only with explicit evidence-strength labels.
- Code completion is not proof of recovered revenue, retention or channel economics.
